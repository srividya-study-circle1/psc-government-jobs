PSC(public service commission) JOBS

Public Service Commission Government Jobs 2024: Indian Public Service Commission (PSC) had been established under Article 315 to 323 of the Constitution of India. The PSC is a authorized agency to conduct Central and State Government Recruitment Examinations for selection of Central Government and State Government Vacancies. A Government of India established State wise Public Service Commission and Union Public Service Commission for selection of various Govt Job Vacancies. The Union Public Service Commission is a Central agency to conducting Central Government Examinations of all India services and higher Central services. The State PSC in every state to conduct local state level exams to state services and to advice on promotions, transfers and disciplinary actions etc.
